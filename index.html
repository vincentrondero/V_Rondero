Auxilium is a crowdfunding app that aims to aid student researchers to enlive their work by giving them the ability to promoteit topeopleby gathering small amounts of funds from a large number of people to finance their work. Through Auxilium and with the help of social media platforms, the project promotion expands the opportunity to be recognized and to be funded for its future advancements. This innovative way of source funding will let their potential and ideas expand for every project they create. This app also helps student researcher(s) project to be marketed once it got its target fund. As long as the project is school-based, Auxilium can promote it for fundraising.
